Background Gun Violence Based on Race/Ethnicity School Shooting Resolutions




In 2022, up until October 31 of this year there has been 41 incidents of school shootings this year that resulted in injuries or deaths. Since 2018, there have been 134 such school shootings. Prior to 2022, the highest number of school shootings with injuries or deaths was last year when there were 35. There were 10 in 2020, and 24 each in 2019 and 2018.

The latest incident happened on November 8, where a student was shot at Ingram High School in Seattle, Washington.
